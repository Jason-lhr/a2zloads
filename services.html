Our Dispatch Services

At A2ZLoads, we offer reliable and cost-effective dispatch services for various types of trucks to meet your logistics needs. Below is a breakdown of our dispatch services:

  • Dry Van Truck - Ideal for general freight, ensuring safe and timely deliveries across regions.
  • Box Truck - Smaller, cost-effective solutions for urban and local deliveries.
  • Power Only - We provide the tractor and a skilled driver to move your trailer safely.
  • Flatbed Truck - Perfect for oversized or heavy equipment that requires open space for loading/unloading.
  • Reefer Truck - Maintain the perfect temperature for your perishable goods with our refrigerated trucks.
